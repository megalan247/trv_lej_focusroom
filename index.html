To book a desk, simply tap one of the desks to the right so they turn red. This means the desk is booked and it will remain booked for 1 hour from checkin.

If you want to extend the booking, tap the desk again to extend in 30 minute intervals.

Once you are finished with the room, tap the "Checkout" button on the desk to checkout and free the desk for another person.
